Galactic Radio Telescope

This project aims to:

  • Register Galaxies across the universe, enabling users to find a Galaxy and a training resources near to them
  • Provide a global tool index and search, connecting users and their research data with a Galaxy that can help them
  • Collect aggregate metrics from Galaxy servers for statistical analysis and optimisation of all Galaxies
